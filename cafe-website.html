Our Story

A Café Built on Love & Fresh Food

Zara's Café started as a small dream in 2019 — a place where the neighborhood could gather, slow down, and enjoy food made with real ingredients.

Every recipe is crafted in-house. Every cup is brewed to order. We believe great food doesn't need to be complicated — just honest and fresh.

100% locally sourced ingredients
Freshly baked every single morning
No artificial flavors or preservatives
Friendly staff, always welcoming
